Memory

If we were to lose all of our memories, do we then cease to exist? Are we then reborn? What makes us, us? Then, if after a while our memories return to us, does that new person that was born then cease to exist as the original is now back? Or are we the same now, a combination of two experiences?

Fading

I think, continuing off of memory, I'd prefer to lose my body than my mind, if I had to choose. Although in either case I would prefer to just die, I'd much rather retain myself than be in top shape. We may become 'different' people due to extreme circumstances, but I think losing all of your memories and having your personality altered due to a disease is much more drastic.
